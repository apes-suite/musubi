public subroutine applySrc_absorbLayerIncomp(fun, inState, outState, neigh, auxField, nPdfSize, iLevel, varSys, time, phyConvFac, derVarPos)

Update state with source variable "absorb_layer". absorb_layer is used to absorb the flow and gradually reduce the flow quantities like pressure and velocity to a fixed value for incompressible model. It is based on: Xu, H., & Sagaut, P. (2013). Analysis of the absorbing layers for the weakly-compressible lattice Boltzmann methods. Journal of Computational Physics, 245(x), 14â€“42.
